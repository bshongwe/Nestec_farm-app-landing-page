what is Nestec?


Nestec is an agriculture app whose aim is to improve agricultural practices faced by South African farmers through its cutting edge tech stack.
The app employs agriculture news content APIs and weather forecast APIs, helps the user manage their farm using cutting edge pest and disease detection
AI technology.

who can use Nestec?


Nestec was mainly developed for farmers in the Vaal, whose target market is the Jo'burg Market. However, its development spans to the entire Republic of South Africa.
Moreover, its user friendly interface allows ease of use by anyone, regardless of tech and agriculture background.
